About Lalaloopsy!

•Lalaloopsy is a series of "fashionable and magical" dolls created by Amandine Consorti.

•Lalaloopsy is owned by MGA entertainment. Their focus is creating magical rag dolls that come to life after they are completed.

•In order to promote the dolls, Amandine Consorti even made a show centered around the lives of the Lalaloopsy dolls!

Achievements

Lalaloopsy has accomplished many things within their time in MGA entertainment. Some of these achievements include:

•Winning the People's Play Award for best large doll in 2010

•Creating over 150 Lalaloopsy characters

•Partnering with the American Red Cross to encourage blood donations through the release of a nurse-themed Lalaloopy doll
